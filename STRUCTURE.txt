KEY WORKFLOWS
─────────────

💾 TO REFRESH DATA:
   1. Export new files → place in _source-data/
   2. Process/clean → save to data/
   3. Update dashboard data files in dashboards/
   4. Deploy dashboards/ folder to Netlify

📖 TO UNDERSTAND THE DATA:
   • Read: _source-data/INDEX.md (what's available)
   • Read: README.md (data types, sources, formats)
   • Check: _research/papers/ (frameworks & analysis)

🔧 TO DEPLOY DASHBOARD:
   • Drag dashboards/ folder → netlify.com/drop
   • (Keep swalwell_dashboard copy.html as the active version)

🔍 TO FIND SOMETHING:
   • Active data?        → Look in dashboards/ or data/
   • Raw exports?        → Look in _source-data/
   • Policy guidelines?  → Look in _reference/House Admin/
   • Research source?    → Look in _research/papers/
   • Old code/library?   → Look in _archive/

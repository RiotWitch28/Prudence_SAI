⚑ Childcare Payments Routed Through Campaign Funds
NOTABLE
FEC source files show childcare payments disbursed through both the Presidential committee (C00701698) and potentially the PAC. In the Presidential CSV, a $3,625 payment to Barbosa, Amanda is classified as "childcare." FEC rules permit campaign childcare reimbursements in limited circumstances, but the routing of personal childcare costs through campaign committees — particularly a presidential campaign that ran only 87 days — is an area flagged for further review. This contributes to the data discrepancy between dashboard figures and source files.

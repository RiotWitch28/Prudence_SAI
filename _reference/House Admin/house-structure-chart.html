Congressional Caucuses, Conferences & Groups

Official Party Organizations

Republican — Official
House Republican Conference
All House Republicans; sets caucus rules, elects leadership, develops policy platform. Chaired by elected Conference Chair.
Democrat — Official
House Democratic Caucus
All House Democrats; ratifies leadership, sets party agenda, manages communications. Chaired by elected Caucus Chair.
Republican — Campaign
National Republican Congressional Committee (NRCC)
Elects House Republicans; recruitment, polling, TV buys, opposition research
Democrat — Campaign
Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee (DCCC)
Elects House Democrats; candidate recruitment, data, ad spending

Ideological & Intra-Party Caucuses

GOP — Hard Right

Republican Study Committee (RSC)

# Reference Documents

Documents, guidelines, and analysis reports used as reference during analysis and feature development.

## House Administration Guidelines
**Folder: `House Admin/`**
- Official House Member & Committee Office handbooks
- Telework policies, HR practices, communications standards
- Legislative operations and constituent service guidance
- Current rules and compliance frameworks

**Key Files:**
- `final-member-handbook-*.pdf` — Official House rules and procedures
- `shared-employee-manual-*.pdf` — HR and workplace policies
- `2024-User-s-Guide-for-Member---Committee-Offices.pdf` — Day-to-day operations guide

## Vendor & Spending Analysis
- **`Swalwell Hotel Vendors 2.xlsx`** — Analysis of hotel and travel vendors used by the office
- **`swalwell vendor profile report.pdf`** — Comprehensive vendor spending patterns
- **`Swalwell Legal Fees Report.docx`** — Legal expenses and outside counsel tracking

---

## How These Are Used

- **House handbooks** → Validate MRA spending against Member Handbook rules
- **HR policies** → Benchmark staffing structure against administrative guidelines
- **Vendor reports** → Cross-reference spending with vendors in dashboard
